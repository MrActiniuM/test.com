3 February 2024: Warrah Lookout, Mt. Ettalong, Ettalong Beach

Wakeup

So, it was the early morning of 3 February, my birthday!

I expected my father to be at AVIS to be renting a car.

And I was right! My father was nowhere to be seen, and his possessions were also nowhere to be seen. He must be at AVIS, I thought. My family had breakfast, and almost immediately after we finished, we heard a knock on the door. It was my father, and the car he rented was a Hybrid Toyota Corolla.

At this time, I had a weird habit of listing our rented cars by their plate numbers. This Corolla had a plate number of 2I3.FT3.

Travel Time!

Now, we packed our bags and packed our supplies. Then, we went to 2I3.FT3.

It was a very nice car, but the big problem was it didn't have a USB port to attach our hard disk drive (HDD). So we just sucked it up and used Bluetooth to connect music with my father's phone.

Now, we were firstly bound for Lidcombe Shopping Centre, to pick up my birthday cake at Breadtop. Only my father entered the store, and My mother, brother and I stayed in 2I3.FT3.

After ordering the cake, we drove off to the M1 Pacific Motorway, bound for the lookout near Patonga, Warrah Lookout.

Exiting the M1


Just after we crossed the oddly-named Mooney Mooney Creek on Mooney Mooney Bridge, we took the ramp off the M1 to enter the A49 Central Coast Highway. Finally, it was a big relief to get out of the noisy highway ambience, and in a few minutes, we turned on Woy Woy Road, headed for obviously, Woy Woy!

After traversing along Woy Woy Road for several kilometres, we bypassed Mt. Ettalong
